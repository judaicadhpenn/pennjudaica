Penn Judaica Portal — Beta. Visual scheme informed by the cover of Milgroym (Berlin, 1922).
Records and images are real and openly licensed, served live from Penn's OPenn: the Benjamin Zucker Family Ketubah Collection, the Lawrence J. Schoenberg Manuscripts, the Cairo Genizah Collection (CAJS / Halper), and the records of Congregation Mikveh Israel. The data layer (data.js) stands in for a server-side harvest of Penn's catalog, Colenda, OPenn & finding aids. Design concept — not an official Penn Libraries service.
